## Instagram Basic Display API

#### Off-Facebook Activity

*Applies to all versions.*

Users who [clear their history or turn off future activity](https://www.facebook.com/help/2207256696182627/) will invalidate their access tokens. In addition, users who have turned off future activity and who attempt to use the [authorization window](instagram-platform/changelog.md) will be prompted to turn on future activity in order to use your app.
