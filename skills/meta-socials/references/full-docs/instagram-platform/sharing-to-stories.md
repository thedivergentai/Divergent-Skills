# Sharing to Stories

You can integrate sharing into your Android and iOS apps so that users can share your content as an Instagram story. To create a new app, see [Getting Started with the Facebook SDK for Android](android/getting-started.md) and [Getting Started with the Facebook SDK for iOS](ios/getting-started.md).

Beginning in January 2023, you must provide a Facebook AppID to share content to Instagram Stories. For more information, see [Introducing an important update to Instagram Sharing to Stories](/blog/post/2022/10/10/introducing-important-update-to-Instagram-sharing-to-stories/). If you don't provide an AppID, your users see the error message "The app you shared from doesn't currently support sharing to Stories" when they attempt to share their content to Instagram. To find your App ID, see [Get Your App ID (Android)](android/getting-started.md#app-id) and [Get Your App ID (iOS)](ios/getting-started.md#app-id).
